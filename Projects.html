Blind Stick

We have developed a Smart BlindStick, an exciting and innovative college Engineering clinics project designed to enhance the independence and safety of visually impaired individuals. This intelligent mobility aid incorporates cutting-edge technologies to provide a comprehensive solution for the visually impaired. Equipped with ultrasonic sensors, water sensors, vibrating motors, alert buzzers,dark sensors, leds, and gyroscope sensors, the Smart BlindStick detects obstacles in the user's path and signals their presence to the people around them. Thereby, offering real-time feedback through haptic vibrations, audible alerts as well as automatic light activation in dark and alert generation during emergency situation. Its advanced GPS navigation system assists users in seamless wayfinding, ensuring accurate location awareness. With a lightweight and ergonomic design, the Smart BlindStick prioritizes user comfort and convenience. This project represents a commitment to inclusivity and accessibility, empowering individuals with visual impairments to navigate their surroundings with greater confidence and autonomy.

Food Quality Detector

We have developed a Food Quality Detector, an exciting and innovative college Engineering project aimed at ensuring food safety and quality. This intelligent system leverages advanced technologies to provide a comprehensive solution for detecting and analyzing the quality of food products. Equipped with sensors that monitor factors like temperature, humidity, gas levels, and pH, the Food Quality Detector identifies signs of spoilage, contamination, or degradation in real-time. The device offers instant feedback through visual indicators and alerts, helping users make informed decisions about the safety and freshness of their food. With its compact and user-friendly design, the Food Quality Detector is easy to use in various settings, from home kitchens to large-scale food production facilities. This project represents our commitment to public health and food safety, empowering consumers and businesses alike to maintain high standards of food quality with confidence.
